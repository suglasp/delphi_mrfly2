Fly2 - slaughter in the rain; a nasty 2nd play on the game "Fly - Giant Breaker".
The basics are still the same, try to catch Mr Fly.

*** note: this game can cause heavy mousebutton damage, by playing this game to long ... ;) ***

New to Fly2:
* Animations
* External configuration program
* Powerup's
* Renderd game menu
* Better score
* New AI

Powerup's and gameplay:
Some new things are added to Fly2.
Score: One of them, is the score. Not only a nice draw'n score counter, but the score depends also on the difficulty settings.
Clouds: As these crappy things move, Mr Fly can hide behind them
Health to Mr Fly: Depends on the difficulty settings, Mr Fly has an internal health system in Fly2. you must click more then once to get Mr Fly.
Healthpack: Only one at at a time available, but if Mr Fly catch this, he gets full health!
Teleporting: Depends on the difficulty setting. Mr Fly teleports him self now (not when you click on him like in Fly - GB). The harder the difficulty setting, the more Mr fly will teleport.

Hints and tips:
1) You can work with your 2 mouse buttons (or even 3 if you have a mouse like that)
2) If Mr Fly losses his helmet (the yellow-red thingy), he is almost dead.
